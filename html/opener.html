The Opener Tool

In May of 2018, the Tampa Bay Rays debuted a strategy known as The Opener by deploying a relief pitcher to pitch the first inning before going to the regularly scheduled starter. Following this start, the question became not if it would be tried again, but who would be the next to do so? This tool should help with figuring that out.

So, what is the making for a good Opener candidate? Basically, we are looking for starting pitchers who struggle often in the 1st inning to be replaced by a quality reliever on the same team. This tool attempts to find relief pitcher candidates with a wOBA less than 0.250 and starting pitcher candidates with a 1st inning wOBA greater than 0.350. The Opener Tool allows for filtering on team and opposing lineup handedness.
