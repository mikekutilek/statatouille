Fantasy Points Tool

Gaining popularity every day, fantasy sports have become a staple of the sports gambling industry. Sports betting is spreading across casinos throughout the country and apps like DraftKings and FanDuel are increasingly popular. Everyone is looking for an edge in these games to earn themselves a shot at millions. Fortunately, we can utilize the data at our fingertips to gain this edge.

This tool is designed to give fantasy players a better way to manage their teams. Instead of looking at boxscores and back of the baseball card statistics like touchdowns, goals, pitcher wins, etc., we should be looking at rate statistics, trends, and opportunity. The tool looks at the most important underlying statistics for each league and compares it to the fantasy points for that player. The idea is to project future performance and identify buy low candidates and smart waiver wire pickups in your league.

All data provided by Hockey Reference, Pro Football Reference, AirYards.com, Baseball Savant, Baseball Reference, and Fangraphs
